The most underestimated skill gap for women in business today isn't leadership or networking — it's tech fluency. I want WIB's L&D to close that gap, one session at a time.

My unique proposition

Tech isn't a domain. It's a language — and every woman in business needs to speak it.

From reading a dashboard to understanding AI tools, negotiating SaaS contracts, or decoding a product roadmap — tech literacy is the new business literacy. I'll build an L&D program that makes this accessible, practical, and relevant to wherever our members are headed.

🏢

Corporate Navigation Track

Workshops on thriving in corporate environments — from decoding org politics to owning your career trajectory with data and confidence.

🚀

Incubation Track

Structured sessions to help members ideate and launch real business ideas — from problem identification to lean validation and pitching.
